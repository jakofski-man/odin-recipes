Marry me chicken!

Description

Marry Me Chicken is creamy, juicy, and full of flavor! It’s said that this chicken dinner is so good that if you make it for your partner, a marriage proposal will be his next question.

This creamy chicken is also family-friendly, and definitely a crowd-pleaser! All you need to make this wonderful dish is just a few ingredients and a bit of your time.

Ingredients

  • 600 g chicken thighs
  • 0,5 ts salt
  • 0,5 ts pepper
  • 1 ts paprika powder
  • 2 tbs neutral oil
  • 2 finely chopped onions
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbs dried oregano
  • 0,5 ts chili flakes
  • 1 tbs tomato paste
  • 4 tbs sundried tomatoes
  • 4 dl chicken stock
  • 3 dl heavy cream
  • 50 g parmesan
  • 100 g fresh spinach
  • juice and zest of 0.25 lemon
  • 2 tbs fresh basil leaves

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into big pieces. Pat dry and season well with salt, pepper and paprika.
  2. Heat a large frying pan or cast iron skillet over medium-high heat. Add oil and a knob of butter. Fry the chicken pieces for 1-2 minutes on each side until golden brown. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.
  3. Lower the heat a little. Add a little more oil, if the pan is dry. Add the onions and fry until soft and shiny. Add garlic, oregano, chili flakes, a splash of tomato paste and sun-dried tomatoes. Let it fry for a minute.
  4. Add chicken stock and bring to a boil, let it reduce slightly.
  5. Turn down the heat and stir in the heavy cream and parmesan. Let it simmer for a while, until the sauce thickens. You can also add some spinach, if you like a little extra green. Let it cook a little in the sauce before continuing.
  6. Add the chicken back in and simmer until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce has thickened, about 5-7 minutes. Season with lemon juice and grated lemon zest, a little salt and pepper, and top with basil leaves just before serving.
